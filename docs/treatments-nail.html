Nail Treatments

Powder Polish

  • Duration: 45-60min
  • Cost: £21

Cuccio - Powder Polish Cuccio pro powder polish utilises new technology to produce a fantastic way of applying permanent colour to the nail and offers a great alternative to gel polish. The application utilises specially formulated, medical grade adhesive gel with state of the art brush on activator and very finely milled acrylic powders to create beautiful nails that last a minimum of 14 days. It is the perfect treatment for clients who have weak or damaged nails, oily nail beds, nail biters, or clients who simply need something a little stronger than gel. The results are fabulous, nails are strengthened and there are lots of colours and glitters to choose from.
